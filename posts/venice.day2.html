While looking for a place to stay and asking about a campsite we got responses like: “yes, yes there is one just 20 km away.” (Translation: bike for another 1.5h and you're there!) At that point Olga was desperate and decided to test our chances with the locals by asking a nice looking lady for recommendations for a cheap hotel or somewhere we could pitch our tent. We attempted italian-english exchange of words along the lines of “hotel, cheap, posto dormitore, gardenio...”. The result was totally unexpected and proved to us once more that truly kind people still exist in this world.
